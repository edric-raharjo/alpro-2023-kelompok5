Where's Wally

"Where's Wally?" (also known as "Where's Waldo?" in North America) is a series of children's puzzle books created by English illustrator Martin Handford

The books consist of a series of detailed, double-page spread illustrations depicting large crowds of people doing a variety of things at a given location. The reader's challenge is to find a specific character named Wally (Waldo in North America), who is hidden within the scene.
